Quesadilla Burgers

Description

The Quesadilla Burger is a great combination between American and Mexican cuisine. With your choice of cheese and toppings, this tortilla meets beef combination will blow you away.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1/4 cup ranch dressing
  • 1/4 cup taco sauce
  • 4(4 ounce) hamburger patties
  • 1 dash Worcestershire suace
  • 8 flour tortillas
  • 2 cups shredder sharp cheddar cheese
  • 2 cups shredded colby-jack cheese
  • 1/2 head lettuce, shredded
  • 3 tablespoons picante sauce

Steps

  1. Mix sour cream, ranch, and taco sauce in a bowl until smooth. Cover bowl and refrigerate for flavors to blend.
  2. Preheat an outdoor grill to medium-high heat
  3. Cook hamburger patties for 5 minutes, seasoning with Worcestershire sauce. Flip patties. Arrange tortillas on grill and lower heat under tortillas. Add cheddar and colby-jack cheese to tortillas; cook until cheese melts, 3 minutes. Continue cooking patties until cooked through. ,about 5 minutes more.
  4. Spread southwest sauce onto 1/2 of the tortillas; top with lettuce and picante sauce. Place 1 hamburger patty on top of picante sauce layer and top patty with a tortilla.
